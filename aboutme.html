ABOUT ME RESUME ☰
Hi,  I’m Min young kim, a 4th  year at Ewha womans university, studying Sculpture and Industrial Design.
I am both an artist and a designer. As the diversity of art grows, many people differentiate the artist and the design, others say the artist and their design are one. I am between those two opinions Majoring in sculpture and industrial design, I both and combine and separate these two. I have the advantage of using the best of both worlds.   I also have a large interest in colour theory
