WISH
Personal Work, 2017

WHY DID I MAKE "WISH"?
I am interested in clay work and photography. I have a sentimental story with my mom and her folding paper cranes. When I was going through a difficult time, my mom told me that it was difficult because I am 20. She too had faced hardship at my age and paper cranes had consoled her. My mom gifted 1000 individually folded paper cranes. For me, this was an example of mother's love.
In Korean, paper cranes means 'dreams come true' and my mom's gift got me through hard times that allowed me to achieve my dreams.
