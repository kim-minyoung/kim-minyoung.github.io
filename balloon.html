BALLOON PROJECT
Personal Work, 2017

WORK EXPLANATION
This project started in order to capture unrealism. There are times when we want to escape reality. During those times, we dream of an unrealistic world. But is there such things as an unrealistic world? Is the unrealistic world an asylum for those in the real world?
Inspired by the movie 'Up', this artwork represents a scene from 'Up' where hanging on balloons allows us to get closer to our dreams
